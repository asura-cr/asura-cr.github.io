Understanding Heart Conditions

AS

Aortic Stenosis - Narrowing of the aortic valve opening, restricting blood flow from the heart to the aorta.

AR

Aortic Regurgitation - Leaky aortic valve allowing blood to flow backward into the left ventricle.

MR

Mitral Regurgitation - Leaky mitral valve causing blood to flow backward into the left atrium.

MS

Mitral Stenosis - Narrowing of the mitral valve opening, restricting blood flow to the left ventricle.

MVP

Mitral Valve Prolapse - Improper closure of the mitral valve leaflets during heart contraction.
